New Zealand Roads

16th October 2014 ~ QGIS

This is a quick map showing New Zealand roads from the Land Information New Zealand Mainland Roads Centrelines (1:250k) dataset (and nothing else), inspired by this map of U.S. Roads. Road centrelines are symbolized with lighter to darker shades of blue based on the data lanes attribute.
